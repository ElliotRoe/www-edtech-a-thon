title: My Algebra students need adaptive mental math practice that challenges everyone at the right level.
---

Many high school Algebra students are missing foundational addition, subtraction, and multiplication skills. Other students already know those facts and need more advanced mental math practice, like solving multi-step equations in their head.

Proposed solution: an adaptive Chromebook game that starts with basic facts and increases difficulty as students improve, with points, badges, unlockables, and a fair leaderboard.
