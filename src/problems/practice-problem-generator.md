title: I need to quickly generate assessment-style math practice problems with specific answer constraints.
---

When preparing for a test, I want to give my students several practice problems similar to what they will see on the assessment. Generating new problems takes a very long time, especially when I need math problems where the answer follows certain constraints, such as no decimals or no negatives.

Proposed solution: a problem generator where teachers input an original question plus the rules that new practice problems need to follow.
